There are two types of resources on the basis of origin-
  • Biotic Resources
  • Abiotic Resources
There are two types of resources on the basis of ownership-
  • Individual resources
  • Community owned resources
There are four type of resources on the basis of status of development-
  • Potential Resources
  • Developed Resources
  • Stock
  • Reserves
There are two types of resources on the basis of exhaustibility-
  • Renewable resources
  • Non-renewable resources
